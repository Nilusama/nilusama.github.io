Recurring Event

Join us at Full Grip Games every other Thursday starting at 6:30 PM in Akron, Ohio. All events are proxy-friendly, gold-border friendly, and reprint-friendly. $10 entry fee paid back in store credit to top earners. Join their Discord.

Major Premodern Event

Our first major MTG Premodern event at Full Grip Games will be on October 4th at 1 PM.

  • $30 entry; store credit to top performers after Swiss rounds + top-cut playoff.
  • Gold Bordered prizes for top players:
    • 1st Place: GB Gaea's Cradle
    • 2nd Place: GB Mud Bundle (3 Grim Monolith, 4 Wasteland)
    • 3rd/4th Place: GB Choice: 3 Force of Will or 1 Survival of the Fittest
  • Events follow the official PreModern banlist.
