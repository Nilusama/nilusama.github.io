Welcome

Welcome to the official Akron Blackblades website. Here you'll find updates, events, and community info for Premodern MTG players in Akron and Cleveland, Ohio. Join us to celebrate classic Magic gameplay and nostalgia from the late 90s and early 2000s.

All events are proxy-friendly (cards must be legible), gold-border friendly, and reprint-friendly.
